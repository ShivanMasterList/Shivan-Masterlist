Shivan

At first sight they can be reminiscent of a terrestrial dinosaur but they are indeed a flying species! Shivans are made on the simple consept of dragons with their wings on the head.

Powerful wings growing from their head allows them to propel themselves up to 70ft off the ground in an instant when threatened.

Their tail has a small but strong pair of wing allowing them to keep stability in flight. the line of spikes at the end of their tail are used to intimidate predators and rivals.

The big spikes on their tail wing tip and on top of their head wings allow them to split air easily and increase their flight speed.

The sharp horn on their nose allows them to dig into the ground to find food, as well as open hard fruits and shells.

The can stand on 2 or 4 legs.

Shivany

Shivany look similar to Shivans, the only differences reside on their wings and fur. Shivanys furs are made of soft materiels such as clouds, wool, cotton, etc.

The wings have the same mutations as Shivans but are covered in feathers instead of scales.

Since Shivany's spend most of their lives high in the sky they are less affected by the gravity, meaning that they tend to grow bigger than an average Shivan

Shivanys are highly intelligent and social creatures. Unlike Shivans they really enjoy human company, preffering to be around children the most. It's said that Shivanys with the Dream Catcher trait can make their wishes come true.

Shivanys put a lot of effort into their apperances, they can be extremly narcissistic at times.
